üéÆ How to Play

  • Desktop: Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move the farmer
  • Mobile: Use the virtual joystick to control movement
  • Auto-Pathing: Check the box to let AI handle movement automatically
  • Combat: The farmer automatically throws axes at nearby enemies
  • Pickups: Walk over green orbs (XP) and hearts to collect them
  • Leveling: Collect XP to level up and get stronger
  • Shop: Every 5 levels, choose upgrades to improve your character
  • Survival: Avoid enemies and try to survive as long as possible!

üéØ Game Features

  • AI Playtesting: The game runs automatically with AI-controlled movement for testing
  • Mobile Support: Touch controls with virtual joystick and action buttons
  • Progressive Difficulty: Enemies spawn faster and become stronger over time
  • RPG Elements: Level up, collect XP, and choose upgrades
  • Performance Monitoring: Built-in memory management and performance tracking
  • Headless Mode: Can run without UI for testing and simulation
